macOS · Swift · Local AI

Chat with AI,
completely offline.

Astex is a native macOS app that gives you a clean, modern chat interface for local language models — powered by Ollama, with no data ever leaving your machine.

↓ Download Latest View on GitHub
🔒

Fully Private

All inference runs locally via Ollama. Your conversations never leave your Mac.

💬

Persistent History

Chats are saved automatically with SwiftData so you can pick up where you left off.

✨

Smart Titles

Conversations are automatically titled based on context — no manual labelling needed.

📦

Model Management

Pull, load, and delete Ollama models directly from within the app.

✈️

Works Offline

No internet required once your models are installed — ideal for air-gapped environments.

🍎

Native Swift

Built entirely in Swift for a fluid, platform-native experience on macOS.

Requirements

macOS 26+ Ollama installed Apple Silicon or Intel
